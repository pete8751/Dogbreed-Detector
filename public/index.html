Woof Detector

Instructions:

The model only accepts JPEG or PNG files

Drag and and drop an image of a dog into the box, or click browse to upload a photo from your computer. Next, simply click Analyze, and the app will then tell you the breed of the dog! Click on the detailed predictions dropdown to see the models top 5 predictions.

To find out more about the machine learning model, how it was built, and how it works, please see the "Documentation" page. To find out about deployment, and see code for the model and the app, please navigate to the github repo, linked in the footer.
